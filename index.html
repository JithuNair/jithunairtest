About Me

Fortunate enough to be born in Kerala and raised across India I am a polyglot (6 languages - going on 7). I currently work for Merkle (a Dentsu Company) performing analytics for a large Fashion Brand based out of France.

  • Hobbies: Photography, Reading (High Fantasy, Science Fiction), Video Games, Riding, Hiking, Trail running
  • I love cinematography if I wasn't an engineer, this would definitely be my alternate career.

In Highschool (Loyola School, Bhubaneswar) I realised my love for computers and pursued a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from ITER, Bhubaneswar. I was recruited as a Junior Data Scientist(2018) during my final semester in college and since then have had the pleasure of working with a plethora of Giants across various sectors: Pharma, Insurance, Automobile, Media, Social Media, Fashion and Lifestyle, Retail, Banking. I have designed end to end solutions meaning everything from Data retrieval to pipelines and dashboards. It gives me immense pleasure being a jack of all traders across all the projects I work on since it exposes me to so many technologies. However I felt something was lacking in just the industry experience and hence decided to pursue a Masters from BITS, Pilani in Data Science and Engineering. It is a full time course alongside my day job but I love the challange every bit as much as I love my work.
